How to Play

The objective of the game is to move the red block all the way to the exit at the right of the board.

You have to clear the path and move the blocks out of the way.

Vertical blocks can only move up and down. Horizontal blocks can only move left and right.

Try to unblock the red block in as few moves as possible!
